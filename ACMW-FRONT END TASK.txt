GUYS THE TASK FOR FRONT END DEVELEOPMENT IS:
***PREPARE A SIMPLE PORTFOLIO WEBSITE OF YOURSELF.***
YOUR PORTFOLIO NEED NOT BE MORE COMPLEX RATHER IT CAN BE SIMPLE AND ATTRACTING.YOU NEED NOT ADD ANIMATIONS FOR YOUR WEBSITE.

*YOUR MUST MAKE YOUR PORTFOLIO USING : 
     1.HTML-YOU CAN ALSO MAKE DIFFERENT HTML PAGES WITH IN YOUR MAIN PAGE BY USING NAVIGATION BUTTONS
     2.CSS
     3,BASIC JAVASCRIPT
THESE ARE THE MUST FOR YOUR WEBSITE.

*YOUR PORTFOLIO MUST INCLUDE:
1.NAVIGATION BAR
2.NAVIGATION BUTTON FOR DIFFERENT PAGES LIKE HOME,ABOUT,CONTACT,SKILLS,EDUCATION.etc.
3.DIFFERENT SECTIONS.
4.YOUR PHOTOGRAPH.
5.YOU CAN ALSO HAVE BACKGROUND IMAGES OR BANNERS FOR YOUR WEBSITE.
6.YOU CAN ALSO ADD PICTURES OF YOUR ACHIEVEMENTS LIKE CERTIFICATES OR IF YOU HAVE PAINTING AS YOUR INTEREST YOU CAN ADD YOUR WORK IN YOUR PORTFOLIO.
 YOU CAN ALSO ADD MANY IDEAS ON YOUR OWN TO CREATE YOUR WEBSITE EFFECTIVELY.
